How We Work

Our Approach to guiding students from skill development to creating an Online Presence increasing employability

01

Awareness and Outreach

Identify target school or communities to approach clients, create promotional materials highliting skillUp's benefits, conduct awareness sessions or webinars to introduce skillUp tp clients/students.

02

Registration and Assessment

Create an online/Physical registration form for interested students and assess students' skills and interests through a survey or quiz

03

Skill Categorization & Development

Schedule classes based on skill categories and student availability. Ensure classes have a student-to-instructor ratio. Instructors(Facilitators) mentor students in their assigned classes to work on projects and assignments

04

Portfolio Development & Online Presence

Guide students to creating a professional online presence(e.g, LinkedIn, Personal Website)

05

Job Placement

Provide career guidance and job search support, offer resume-building workshops, interview preparation and connects studenst with potential employers/Job opportunities

06

Ongoing Support

Continue to provide support & mentorship to students after course completion, encourage them to join SkillUp's alumni networkfor networking and collect feedbacks to improve SkillUp's Programs
